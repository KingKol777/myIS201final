Cafe Racer

Cafe racer is a type of motorcycle that was developed in the 1950s.

History

The cafe racer culture originated in London during the 1950s and 1960s, when motorcycle enthusiasts would customize their bikes to make them faster and more agile, so they could be ridden to and from coffee shops.

  1. Cafe racers were first developed in London.
  2. They were popular among motorcycle enthusiasts in the 1950s and 1960s.
    • Cafe racers were often customized for speed and agility.
